PDF::LogoFile

Inabainisha njia kwenda kwenye faili kwa ajili ya nembo katika kichwa cha ukurasa (gif|jpg|png, 700 x 100 pikseli).

This setting can not be deactivated.

Thamani chaguo-msingi: 

--- <OTRS_CONFIG_Home>/var/logo-otrs.png

PDF::MaxPages

Inafafanua namba ya upeo wa juu ya kurasa lwa kila faili la PDF.

This setting can not be deactivated.

Thamani chaguo-msingi: 

--- '100'

PDF::PageSize

Inafafanua kiwango cha ukubwa wa kurasa za PDF.

This setting can not be deactivated.

Thamani chaguo-msingi: 

--- a4
