Processing of service requests

Within OTRS, service requests and incidents are both regarded as tickets and initially processed equally. They are distinguishable from disruptions because of the ticket type Incident::Service Request.

Another difference, the SLA relevant times, is explained in greater detail in the service levels and service level agreements section.
